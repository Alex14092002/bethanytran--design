Brief: Red Bull Basement empowers student innovators to drive positive change through tech solutions. This competition enables a chance to discover first-hand how some of the world’s biggest change-makers disrupt their scene.

Approach: In 2021 I entered the New Zealand RedBull Basement competition. With my project partner, our concept won the National Championship. This enables us to progress to the international stage of the competition. The problem we were trying to solve related to the isolation of the elderly during the COVID-19 response.

Solution: An idea for senior care from Bethany Tran and John Golena New Zealand ticks the Body & Mind and Smart Cities categories. Our dynamic smartpen, Testament, tracks a person's overall health as they write letters. Testament converts handwriting into a digital format ready to send, while also collecting data on heart rate, blood oxygen levels, and the mood of the message. Receiving the message and information via an app enables distant family members not only to connect but to better track the condition of their elderly relatives.
