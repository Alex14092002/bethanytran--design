Brief: The brief was to create and compile a design solution or creative project that explores your passion within the field of media design.

Approach: This project aims to find the most effective solutions to enhance the shopping experience of blind people through design practice. Multiple types of research and interviews were used to collect data and insights about blind people's behaviors and how they feel. The results collected have shown that although the blind requires assistance, the feeling of constantly needing help makes them feel like they have no power over their decisions.

Solution: A solution has been found to solve their frustration which leads to our idea of making 3D clothing tag. Instead of the flat printed fashion industry tags, we want to evolve the traditional clothing tag into something different that can be accessible for the visually impaired as well.
