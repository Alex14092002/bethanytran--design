DEAR MAMA & PAPA

Brief:



Approach:




Solution:



Brief: How can I use my personal story to inspire children of Asian immigrants who share the same struggle of the inability to express themselves and communicate with their parents about the emotional toll they are experiencing through the use of graphic & product design and storytelling?

Approach: The purpose of this project is to highlight the impact of Dual-Identity on the child of Southeast Asian immigrants through my personal story by creating a tangible platform. This will help enable parents and children to communicate one another's sentimental feelings and emotions in a way that is not speaking face to face making it simpler.

Solution: Dear Mama & Papa is a personal research project that aims to provide individualized listening experiences for Asian immigrant parents and their children to use in order to help them communicate one another’s sentimental feelings and emotions. Users record their messages, which are then stored in a specifically constructed and customized "Memory Box" that can play the audio experience through the built-in speakers.

The Audio Memory Box is a brilliant way to save and keep our loved ones' memories and stories. It embodies the emotions of people, conveying a story or a memory. Listening to someone narrate their stories in their own voice will transport us to different aspects of feelings and events in life. The message will be recorded and archived as an audio diary that can be treasured by future generations.
